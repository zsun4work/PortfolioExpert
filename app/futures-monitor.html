📖 Understanding Futures Basis

Basis = (Futures Price - Spot Price) / Spot Price × 100%

Annualized Cost = Basis × (365 / Days to Expiry)

  • Positive basis (Contango): Futures trade at premium → holding cost
  • Negative basis (Backwardation): Futures trade at discount → potential roll yield

Spot Price Calculations:

  • Nasdaq 100: Direct comparison with ^NDX index
  • Treasury (ZB): Spot derived from ^TYX yield using: Price = 6% coupon / yield × 100
  • Gold: GLD ETF price × 10.7 (GLD holds ~1/10.7 oz per share)
  • Dollar Index: DX-Y.NYB continuous contract as spot reference
